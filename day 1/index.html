Image Of The Day

Icelandic Cyclones

A cyclone is a low-pressure area of winds that spiral inwards.
Although tropical storms most often come to mind, these spiraling storms can also form at mid- and high latitudes.
Two such cyclones formed in tandem south of Iceland in November 2006.
